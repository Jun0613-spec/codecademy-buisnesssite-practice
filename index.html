Cocktail Recipes

Irish Lemonade

How To Make

  1. Fill a high ball glass with ice and pour in 50ml of Duncannon Irish Whiskey.
  2. Top up the glass with a good quality sparkling lemonade.
  3. Stir to mix.

Irish Paloma

How To Make

  1. Rim glass with Chamoy & Tajin.
  2. Build Duncannon Irish Whiskey and lime juice in a glass.
  3. Shake and strain over ice.
  4. Top with Citrus Soda.

Classic Whiskey Sour

How To Make

  1. Fill a shaker with ice, and then add your Dumcannon Irish Whiskey,
    freshly-squeezed lemon juice, sugar syrup, egg white and 3 dashes of Angostura bitters.

  2. Shake and strain into a chilled rocks glass, before garnishing with a lemon twist.
